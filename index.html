About Europol

Europol is the European Union's law enforcement agency combating serious international crime and terrorism. Their mission includes protecting vulnerable populations, including children, online and offline.

Report Illegal Content

If you encounter illegal content online, especially involving the exploitation of children, you can report it safely using the official channels:

  • Report to Europol
  • Report to IWF (UK)
  • Safer Internet Centres
